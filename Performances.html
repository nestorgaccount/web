  • Nestor Gonzalez is a LA/San Diego based Drumline & Front Ensemble Instructor, and Drumset Instructor. His style of teaching is based on building a well rounded understaing of musical styles, and real world applications. His influences include Roy Haynes, Duncan Moore, Don Brewer, Jeff Porcaro, Nick Mason, Johnny Vidacovich, and Brian BLade. Nestor is currently a BFA student at CalArts in the Performer-Composer program. His Perfomance credits include The Coronado Playhouse (Follies, 2019), Center Stage Productions (Merry Poppins (2018), Addams Family (2019), Mamma Mia(2019), Vanguard Productions San Diego (Godspell, 2019), San Diego Jazz Festival (SWC Jazz Choir), Del Mar Fair (La Beat Cantina), and session work around San Diego.
