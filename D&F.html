  • Nestor Gonzalez is an LA/San Diego based Drumline & Front Ensemble Instructor, and Drumset Instructor. His style of teaching is based on building a well rounded understaing of musical styles, and real world applications. His influences include Roy Haynes, Duncan Moore, Don Brewer, Jeff Porcaro, Nick Mason, Johnny Vidacovich, and Brian BLade. Nestor is currently a BFA student at CalArts in the Performer-Composer program. His Instructional credits include Castle Park High School, Southwestern College, and Hart High School.
