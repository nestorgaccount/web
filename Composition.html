  • Nestor's compositions are rooted in story,and the natural world. In his compositions, Nestor explores the idea of sonic scenery, painting an image in the listeners imagination through the use of orchestral composition, field recordings, and improvised percussion. Nestor is currently a BFA student at CalArts in the Performer-Composer program. His Perfomance credits include The Coronado Playhouse (Follies, 2019), Center Stage Productions (Merry Poppins (2018), Addams Family (2019), Mamma Mia(2019), Vanguard Productions San Diego (Godspell, 2019), San Diego Jazz Festival (SWC Jazz Choir), Del Mar Fair (La Beat Cantina), and session work around San Diego.
